High Profile & Reliable UAE National Local Partner

The UAE Companies Law, a UAE national need to act as a “local service agent” for a Limited Liability Company. To comply with the provision of UAE companies’ law, a UAE national need of act as a “Local professional service agent” for LLC.

The local partner must hold majority equity holding of 51% or more where foreign equity in the company may not exceed 49%. The companies often worried about coming into the market and selecting a local partner.

We will arrange you the reliable and multitasking individual who will assist the organization and will not be only a sleeping partner. It’s very important to have a proper local corporate service agent to set up a company, so we want to create awareness for these entrepreneurs and educate foreigners to our country. We provide the channel of communication and networking to the officials because foreigners wouldn’t know the mechanisms of talking to an official here.
