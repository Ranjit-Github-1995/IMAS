Ajman Offshore

Offshore company registration & formation

Ajman offshore company formation was established in the year 2014, since then it is been proved to be one of the best choice of IBC’s. At first level minimum 2 directors are required where shareholder can be designated as director.

Shareholders will decide on capital and for the formation of the company 1 shareholder is needed and formally 1 secretary is needed on which shareholder/director can be designated as secretary where no need of setting up the office

Advantages :
  • Ajman agent itself is official address of the registered offshore company
  • Economical cost company setup
  • Offshore company is allowed to sell and purchase property in UAE
  • Eligible to open bank account in Dubai and use its ports
  • Office, Shops & Warehouses are available
  • Do international business with no restrictions
