Be the Entrepreneur that You Wanted to Be

When you are your own boss, you get to channel the direction of where your career wants to head towards. Starting up a business can be daunting, to say the least. Not only do you have to understand the logistics of the specific field of your business, but also the legalities of the place where you are setting up your business in.

Understanding the Licensing System for Setting up Business in Dubai

It is of utmost importance that you understand the intricacies of the place where you want to establish your business. This is part of the groundwork that you need to lay your attention to. With different places having a different licensing system, Dubai is no exception. With the help of our experts, we help shape up your business no matter which part of the UAE you are located.

    What Bizgate do for the clients?

    The DED is the vital government body for registering and licensing companies who intend to do business in the mainland of Dubai. The DED recently has been updating its rules and regulations to act as a one stop shop service centre for all licenses in mainland Dubai. Today DED provides fast track services compared to most of the government authorities in other emirates or neighbouring Gulf countries.

    • Draft the MOA and AOA
    • Help in obtaining approval from DED
    • Registering the company name
    • Acquiring out an office space
    • Apply for a trade license
