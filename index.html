Our Services at a Glance...

We are a one-stop business solution providers for aspiring entrepreneurs in Dubai, UAE. We are regarded as one of the leading service providers forbusiness setup and corporate PRO services thatcomprises of expert business consultants and PRO team with complete knowledge of the Company formation, PRO Services and UAE laws.

Company Formation

We help aspiring and upcoming business entrepreneurs establish their businesses and companies in the mainland (onshore) as well as in the Freezone and offshore areas of the UAE so that you can work hassle-free without any restrictions within the paradigm of the UAE law. Our highly-efficient team function round-the-clock in providing services to ensure that your business becomes a profitable one in whichever area of the UAE you choose to function in.

UAE National Partner

In Dubai, it is extremely important for a business or a company setup to find the best local sponsor and a local partner. Here, local partner refers to a trustworthy, available, and a responsible local business partner or local service agent. We offer high-profile, educated UAE national partners which help the aspiring business entrepreneurs to enter and explore the UAE market.

Corporate PRO Services

We provide a wide range of comprehensive corporate PRO services to existing companies in the UAE with years of experiences andknowledge and a well-functioning PRO team. We are expert in document clearing and processing in different departments and in taking a big load of liaising with the authority which carries out our client's day-to-day operations smoothly.

Business Center/Office space Solutions

We offer office space facilities to our mainland company clients with a bunch of facilities as per their requirements like Flexi desk, separate office space, PO box no, Landline, and Wifi, among other amenities.

Accounting & Book-Keeping Services

IMAS also takes care of accounting and book-keeping services as an outsource. You don’t need to hire an accountant separately because we will tackle the job for you. This involves handling your invoice, data management, book-keeping, and payroll services, among others.
