Dubai LLC Company Formation – Limited Liability Company

LLC company formation in UAE is most common form of business with the fact that this is only the option which gives you maximum legal ownership i.e. 49% to expatriates for a local business. It is mandatory to have local service agent or sleeping partner with 51%+ of share holding. LLC company formation in Dubai can be formed by a minimum of 2 and a maximum of 50 shareholders whose liability is limited to their shares in the businesses capital.

The typical annual Sponsorship fee for Dubai LLC company formation can run from US $4,200 per year for a small shop to several millions US Dollars for a large multinational company.
